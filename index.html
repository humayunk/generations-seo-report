How SEO Works (Quick Primer)

For context on what this report measures

Keywords are the words and phrases people type into Google when looking for services. When your website content matches those keywords, Google is more likely to show your site in search results.

Rankings/Position refers to where your site appears in Google results. Position 1 is the top result. Positions 1-10 are on the first page. Most clicks go to the first 3-5 results.

Authority Score is Semrush's measure of your website's overall credibility (0-100). New sites start very low. It grows as you earn backlinks from other websites, publish quality content, and build a track record.

Site Health measures technical SEO factors like page speed, broken links, proper tags, and mobile-friendliness. A score of 79% means there are fixable issues that could be hurting rankings.

SEO is a long-term strategy. Changes typically take 30-90 days to fully reflect in rankings. The work we're doing now sets the foundation so that when patients search for care in Standish, MI, Generations Health shows up.
